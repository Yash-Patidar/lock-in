# Lock-In - Ultimate Productivity & Focus App

Lock-In is a comprehensive productivity application designed to help users maximize their focus and accomplish more with less distraction.

## Key Features
- **Pomodoro Timer**: Customizable focus sessions with work/break intervals
- **Task Management**: Add, organize, and track your daily tasks
- **Progress Heatmap**: Visual representation of your productivity streaks
- **Dynamic Themes**: Multiple beautiful themes for personalized experience
- **Keyboard Shortcuts**: Efficient navigation and control
- **Real-time Statistics**: Track your focus sessions and productivity metrics

## Technology Stack
- Next.js 15 with React 19
- TypeScript for type safety
- Tailwind CSS for styling
- Jotai for state management
- Three.js for 3D animations
- GSAP for smooth animations

## Target Users
Professionals, students, developers, freelancers, and anyone looking to improve their productivity and maintain focus during work sessions.
